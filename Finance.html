Market Analysis

Our product is very useful for people who own pools or
beachfront properties in polluted waters.
The Waterumba can easily clean up waters around popular beaches,
allowing a beautiful and clean vacation spot.
In addition, cleaning pools can be very tiresome,
but just place them in a Waterumba and it’ll be spotless within hours!
It can also be purchased to help cleanse the oceans
of the massive amounts of floating trash piles,
which can destroy entire ecosystems and harms wildlife!

Financial Plan

Our Waterumbas cost $500 dollars each,
as they are reusable and the fact that they also run on water.
A regular Roomba that runs on land is usually
around $200-$1000, depending on the model and the features.
We also offer free repairs after the purchase for one year
if the Waterumba is damaged, broken, or not working properly.
We will also hold fundraisers and donations for
local charities or investors to start up our business
and help clean Earth’s waters.
