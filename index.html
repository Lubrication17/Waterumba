Team History

Our team is from California and met
at Palo Alto High over the summer.
We all had an interest in the growing water pollution problem
and wanted to put an end to it.
After we thought of our solution,
a machine called the "Waterumba",
we realized our product would work
both in oceans, lakes, and bays as well as pools, so we decided
to combine both cleaning pools as well as our oceans together.
We had some disagreements on design and logos,
but eventually we settled on our current design choice,
and after many months of hard work,
we have finally finished our Waterumba
and we are ready to present it to you.

Overview

Benefits

The Waterumba is very flexible in whatever aquatic environment you place it in.
We use sensors to avoid walls
and any wildlife while targeting filth.
It can function for very large amounts of time,
as our Waterumba is solar powered.
If a storm were to come and block out the sun,
the Waterumba is stocked with backup batteries
that can last up to 10 hours!
You can change the mode of the Waterumba
on an included app on your device.
The two modes are auto or remote control.
